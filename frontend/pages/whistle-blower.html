Whistle-blower

This secure form allows you to report concerns regarding business violations, suspicious practices, or illegal or unethical conduct related to RSR Group and its subsidiaries.

Your report is confidential. Only our dedicated Legal and Compliance team has access. You are encouraged to remain anonymous and should not disclose any information that could reveal your identity. All reports are handled with strict confidentiality in accordance with Saudi Arabian laws and corporate governance standards.

Please provide detailed information and evidence if possible. Please note:

  • This portal is for reporting suspected fraud, financial misconduct, or illegal acts, not for general feedback on company policies.

  • Do not discuss the report or investigation with other employees or third parties.

  • Your communication is protected under the RSR Group Whistleblower Policy.

  • You will receive a unique case ID and password; please retain them for future correspondence.

  • A team member will respond to your report within 72 hours.
