Al Murooj & Real Estate

Murooj Saudi Riyad Base Real Estate Company is a prominent and well-established real estate development company operating primarily in the Riyadh region of Saudi Arabia. The name "Murooj" (المروج) translates to "meadows" or "pastures" in Arabic, evoking an image of lush, green, and serene environments, which is a core theme in their developments.

The company is a key player in the Kingdom's real estate sector, particularly known for creating large-scale, integrated residential communities that blend modern living with natural landscapes.
